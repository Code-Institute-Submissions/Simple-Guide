Prague

What do you want to do?

  • Eat

    You will not go hungry in Prague. This city offers everything from hearty meat dishes to vegetarian/vegan alternatives of Czech traditional cuisine. The city is filled with unique restaurants and cafes that will satisfy even the most picky eater. The flavour fusion is extraordinary and should be enjoyed with a cold brew of the finest Czech beer.

  • See

    Prague is a medieval city. It is a city that displays some of Europes finest Gothic and Reneissance architecture, with baraque style churches and bridges. While roaming through Prague make sure to always LOOK UP, so you don't miss the astonishing detail and craftmanship of those before us.

  • Events

    Prague hosts an array of events all year around, from food and drink festivals to music and art gatherings. Prague is also recognised for the colourful Easter and Christmas markets. It doesn't matter what time of year you go visit Prague, something is bound to be happening.

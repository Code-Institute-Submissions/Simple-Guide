Prague

What do you want to do?

  • Eat

    Traditional Czech cuisine is very hearty and warming. It is full of texture and flavour. You cannot visit Prague and not have goulash. Traditional goulash is braised beef served with dumplings, saurkraut and OF COURSE a side of delicious Czech beer.

  • See

    Prague is filled with Gothic and Renaissance architecture, as well as, baraque style churches and bridges. Make sure to explore the unique medieval streets with great attention to the astonishing detail. While roaming through Prague make sure to always LOOK UP!

  • Events

    Prague hosts array of events all year around, from food and drink festivals to music and art festivals. Prague is also recognised for the colourful Easter and Christmas markets. It doesn't matter what time of year you go visit Prague, something is bound to be happening.
